Welcome to the Strategic Offense Initiative

Start your own ICBM program, in the comfort of your own home. Build your missiles, program them, and rain down death on your enemies (or friends).

Features

Currently SOI only simulates a fixed rocket, but the following are planned:

  • Build your own missile out of missile parts (warheads, guidance computers, engines, etc.)
  • Program your guidance computers and timers
  • Research new parts for your construction
  • Multiplayer — fight your friends for world domination
  • Defend innocent civilian cities from your enemies missiles
